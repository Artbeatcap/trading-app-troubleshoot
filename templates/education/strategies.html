Options Strategies Guide

Explore foundational income and directional strategies, their risk/reward trade-offs, and when to deploy them.

Long Call

Buy a call when bullish on the underlying.

  • Risk: Premium paid.
  • Reward: Unlimited.
  • Breakeven: Strike + premium.

Long Put

Buy a put when bearish.

  • Risk: Premium paid.
  • Reward: Up to strike − premium.

Covered Call

Sell a call against 100 shares you own to generate income.

  • Risk: Stock upside capped above strike.
  • Reward: Premium + stock gains up to strike.
  • Ideal When: Slightly bullish / neutral.

Cash-Secured Put

Short a put while holding cash to potentially buy stock at strike.

  • Risk: Stock can fall to zero.
  • Reward: Premium income.
  • Ideal When: Slightly bullish / willing to own shares.
